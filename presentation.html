Your Roadmap

Three phases.
Six months.
New title.

This isn't a multi-year transformation. Most PMO leads can make this shift in one focused half-year.

01
Build AI Fluency (Months 1–3)
Take one structured AI course. Run experiments with AI tools. Build a personal mental model of what AI can and can't do.
02
Reframe Your Identity (Months 2–4)
Update your resume, LinkedIn, and how you talk about your work. Start calling yourself an AI change leader — in your own team first.
03
Lead an AI Pilot (Months 4–6)
Sponsor or run one real AI initiative end-to-end. Measure it. Present results. Use it as your proof point and portfolio piece.

Objections Addressed

The three things that
hold PMO leads back —
and the honest reframe.

"I'm not technical"
The role doesn't require you to code. It requires you to evaluate AI outputs and govern programs — which you already do.
"I don't have AI experience"
Neither did anyone 3 years ago. The people hiring for these roles want governance instincts far more than AI credentials.
"I don't know where to start"
You start by running one AI pilot inside your current role. You don't need a new title to begin building the new skillset.
